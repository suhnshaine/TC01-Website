🌟 About Our Class

Welcome to TC01, a dynamic and passionate class section in the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program at FEU Institute of Technology. We are a community of dedicated students who share a common goal: mastering the art and science of web development.

Under the expert guidance of Dr. Joferson Bombasi, we're not just learning to code – we're learning to create digital experiences that matter. Our journey through "Web Design with Client Side Scripting" has been transformative, challenging us to think creatively and solve problems innovatively.

Every project we undertake is an opportunity to push boundaries, explore new technologies, and collaborate as a team. We believe that great web development comes from understanding both the technical aspects and the human experience.
